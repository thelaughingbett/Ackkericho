THE CHURCH IS NOT A BUILDING

The church is the people of God within the world being agents of hope

This vibrant community of action takers and careful stewards of a historic facility are listening to the call of the Spirit and our community and looking for authentic, engaging, progressive, caring, rooted, and justice-seeking ways of being the church in the time of great upheaval.

Gather in Community

Founded in 1976, The Archdeaconary of sotik was first organized to serve the pepole of sotik and kilgoris ( now part of the Archdeaconary of transmara ), it now serves the parishes of : Manaret, Sotik, Bureti ,Saruchat and Bomet.it currently has a 21 clergy membership,15 layreaders and 6 ordained reverends 5 of whom serve the parshes and one under the chaplainacy of moi minaret,

we invite you to fellowship with us at any one of our parishes and know that you will feel blessed
