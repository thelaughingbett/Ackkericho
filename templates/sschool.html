A Children In Christ

One day children were brought to Jesus in the hope that he would lay hands on them and pray over them. The disciples shooed them off. But Jesus intervened: “Let the children alone, don’t prevent them from coming to me. God’s kingdom is made up of people like these.”
Matthew 19 v14

In Matthew 19 v14 Jesus affirms the importance of children and their place in God's kingdom.Jesus captures his profound love and care for children, emphasizing their innocence and purity as models for entering into a relationship with God. Jesus reveals his tender affection for children. His willingness to bless them and spend time with them demonstrates his compassion and desire for a personal relationship with every individual, regardless of their station in life.
